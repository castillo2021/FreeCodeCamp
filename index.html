Steve Jobs

The following image shows Steve Jobs with his daughter.




1971 – Meets Steve Wozniak with whom Apple is later founded.

1975 –Attendance at meetings of the "Homebrew Computer Club" begins, for lovers of the personal computer.

1976 – Jobs and Wozniak raise $1,750 and build their first computer, the Apple I.

1976 –Founds Apple Computer Company with Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. Wayne sells his stake two weeks later.

1980 — –Apple goes public, stock price jumps from $22 to $29 on its first day of trading.

1983 — –Announces "Lisa", the first computer controlled with a mouse. It is a failure in the market.

1985 –Founds Next Inc. to develop computer hardware and software. The company is later renamed as Next Computer Inc.
